Recipe #1

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 2-3
Ingredients Quantity
Campbells Tomatoto Soup 1 can
American Cheese 2-4 slices
White Rice 1 cup

Step 1: Boil 2 cups water
Step 2: Put rice in water and cover for 20 minutes on low
Step 3: Put cup of tomato soup in, then fill the can with water and add that. Stir.
Step 4: Add cheese. Stir again till it melts in.

Recipe #2

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Ramen 1 Packet
Peanut Butter 2-3 Scoops
Siracha (optional) Your discretion

Step 1: Put ramen in bowl with seasoning and 2 cups water
Step 2: Microwave for 3 minutes
Step 3: Take out, and put PB in ramen
Step 4: Mix PB and siracha in broth until thouroughly combined
